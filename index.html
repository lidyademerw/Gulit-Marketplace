ስለ እኛ (About Gulit)

Our Mission

ጉሊት (Gulit) is more than a marketplace. We are a bridge between talented Ethiopian craftswomen and the world. By removing technical barriers, we ensure that every artisan can showcase her work and receive a fair price for her heritage and skill.

How it Works

  • Browse: Discover unique, hand-crafted Ethiopian items.
  • Direct Connect: Call the artisan directly to ask questions.
  • Empower: Your purchase supports local families and preserves our culture.
