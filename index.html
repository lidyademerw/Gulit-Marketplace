ስለ እኛ (About Gulit)

Our Mission

Empowering Ethiopia's Heritage

ጉሊት (Gulit) is a bridge between the ancient skills of Ethiopian craftswomen and the modern global market. Every purchase directly supports a family and preserves a piece of history.

🚚

Fast Delivery

We ship nationwide in Ethiopia and globally via DHL.

🛡️

Safe Payment

Secure transactions via Chapa, Telebirr, and CBE.

Common Questions

How do I contact the artisan?

Once you click "Add to Cart," you can connect directly via our Telegram support for custom orders.

Is international shipping available?

Yes! We use DHL for international orders. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout.
